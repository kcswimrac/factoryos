Mission
Structured engineering should not be a luxury.

Every manufacturer — from a student CubeSat team to a defense contractor — deserves the tools to build with precision, traceability, and confidence. The existing tools are either too expensive, too complex, or too generic. Factory-OS changes that.

We're building the engineering management system that enforces the design cycle — enforced phase gating, structured DOE, requirements traceability, and AI assistance at every step. Not a file vault. Not a checklist app. A discipline engine.

What Factory-OS Is
The TurboTax for hardware engineering.

TurboTax doesn't trust you to remember the tax code — it walks you through every step, enforces what's required, and won't let you file incomplete. Factory-OS does the same for engineering: it enforces the design cycle so teams can't skip critical steps, miss required evidence, or make decisions without documentation.

The system is opinionated by design. Phase gating is strict. Requirements traceability is mandatory. AI assistance is context-aware, not generic. It teaches discipline as teams use it — so the process sticks even when engineers move on.
